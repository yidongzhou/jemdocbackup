Yidong Zhou @ University of Science and Technology of China

I am an undergraduate student of Department of Statistics and Finance at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). I am interested in statistics and computer science, as well as their applications in machine learning and biomedicine.

Besides, I'm the director of welcome party of 2016 in USTC. It's really enjoyable and fulfilling to organize extra-curricular activities. I also enjoy travelling, as you may discover from my album.

I firmly believe that science researchers should bear immense intellectual curiosity about the universe and a high level of motivation to explore the unknown, more importantly, a sense of social responsibility, which encourages them to defend the truth. The pursuit of truth, beauty, as well as hope, has governed my behaviour and lighted up my path. It may not be an easy path, but a path with pride and glory.
