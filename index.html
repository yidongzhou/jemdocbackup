About me

I am an undergraduate student of Department of Statistics and Finance at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). I am interested in statistics and computer science, as well as their applications in biomedicine and machine learning.

Besides, I'm the director of welcome party of 2016 in USTC. It's really enjoyable and fulfilling to organize extra-curricular activities. I also enjoy travelling, as you may discover from my album.

I firmly believe that science researchers should have a sense of social responsibility, which encourages them to defend the truth. The pursuit of truth, beauty, as well as virtue, has governed the behaviour of me. This may not be an easy path, but a path with glory and pride.
